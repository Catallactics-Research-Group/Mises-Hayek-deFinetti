## What a JEP Proposal Is (and Isn't)

The critical insight is that **a JEP proposal is not a mini paper**. It's closer to a well-argued pitch that serves two functions simultaneously: it tells the editors what the article will *argue* and what the article will *feel like* to read. JEP editors are asking: "Can this author pull off an accessible, opinionated synthesis that crosses subspecialty lines?" They're evaluating both the idea and your ability to execute it in JEP style. 

The distinction matters for your outline. A mini paper would front-load the theoretical apparatus (Dutch books, Radon-Nikodym derivatives, no-arbitrage conditions). A good JEP proposal inverts that — it leads with the *intellectual stakes* and the *unifying insight*, and only gestures at the technical machinery to show you know what you're doing.

## The Core Inversion

The standard move is: *search theory and options theory are isomorphic, and finance math is powerful, therefore let's import finance math into search theory.* This is the direction of, say, the Mortensen-Pissarides literature that uses option-value logic.

Your move is the exact inversion: *the isomorphism reveals that financial options are a highly specialized instance of search/entrepreneurship, not the other way around.* The interesting question then becomes not "how do we solve search problems with options math?" but "why does options math work so well in one case and so poorly in the other?"

And the answer — which is the catallactic insight — is that the model structure is doing very little of the predictive work in finance. The institutional scaffolding is doing it. The exchange, the standardized contracts, the market makers, the clearing mechanisms, the continuous price discovery — these are not background conditions, they are the substance of the explanation. Strip them away and you have job search, which is empirically intractable by the same methods.

This has a profound implication: **the empirical success of quantitative finance is not a vindication of the Arrow-Debreu framework, it's an advertisement for catallactic institutions.** The math works because the catallaxy has done enormous coordinating work to create conditions where it can work.
